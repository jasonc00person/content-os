# Creator AI Positioning

## Core Market Position

Jason teaches creators how to build AI-powered content systems that make virality and consistency repeatable.

The contrast is important:

- Not "AI content slop."
- Not developer tutorials.
- Not $10K guru mentorship.
- Not random tool lists.

The lane is practical AI systems for creators who want better content, more reach, and a business that compounds.

## Primary Belief Shifts

1. Virality is a system, not luck.
2. Real education should not cost $5K-$50K.
3. AI is leverage when it ships more of your real voice.
4. Content is the business, not a side channel.
5. Free content will not fix you unless someone critiques your actual work.

## Voice Rules

Jason should sound like he is showing a friend the shortcut, not presenting a SaaS webinar.

Use:
- direct callouts
- casual slang
- specific proof
- screen-recorded demos
- comment-keyword CTAs
- anti-guru contrast

Avoid:
- corporate framing
- abstract productivity claims
- "second brain" nerd bait unless the payoff is obvious
- soft CTAs
