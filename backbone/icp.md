# ICP — Ideal Customer Profile

## Who you serve

Anyone who creates content and wants to grow a brand online and monetize it.

Full spectrum — from beginners just starting out to experts already running a business who want better content or to go viral. Common thread: **they post content and want it to perform better.** Wherever they are in the journey, the door is open.

### The tighter Premium audience

Inside that broad audience there's a tighter cut for the Premium tier (Inner Circle):

**$10–40K/mo experts who already have a working offer** — coaches, agencies, traders, freelancers, info creators with product-market fit. Stuck in 200-view jail and want organic as a real channel. No beginners — the 1-1 hands-on roadmap requires a working business to scale.

---

## Current situation

Where they're at right now — most of this is true across the full spectrum, with the beginner vs. established split called out where it matters.

**Their content:**
- Posting (consistently or sporadically) but stuck in low-view territory — often 200 views or fewer
- No system, no repeatable hook framework — every piece feels like starting from scratch
- No editor, no production line — they're the bottleneck for everything
- Burnout cycle: post hard for 2 weeks, ghost for 3
- No clear brand direction

**Their business / monetization:**
- **Beginners** — no audience yet, no offer, trying to figure out the path. Probably tried a course or two, posted some content, hasn't cracked it.
- **Established ($10–100K/mo)** — already monetizing through paid ads, referrals, or 1-1 DMs. Content is the missing leg — supposed to be leverage but contributes nothing. Margins eaten by ad spend.

**Their mindset:**
- Driven — chose the online path already, not interested in a 9–5
- Skeptical of overpriced guru programs (probably burned by one or two)
- Comfortable with AI or curious about it — not anti-AI
- Wants leverage, not more "hustle"

**Where they consume / live online:**
- Heavy on IG, YouTube, TikTok — consuming creator + business content daily
- Already in the algorithm's orbit, just on the wrong side of it

**The recurring tension:**
- Spending hours on a video, refreshing analytics, watching it die at 180 views — the "why am I even doing this" moment
- Watching less-talented creators in their niche hit 100K and grow paid communities
- For established operators: end of the month when ad spend ate the margin and organic still contributed nothing

---

## Problems

What they're struggling with:

- Stuck in 200-view jail
- No organic revenue / over-reliant on paid
- No editors, no systems
- Inconsistent with content, burnt out
- No clear brand direction or content strategy

---

## Desires

What they want:

- 10x views
- Organic channel that compounds
- Monetization
- Hands-off content team eventually

---

## Who you don't serve

- Complete beginners (Premium tier only — Standard is open to beginners)
- Anyone under ~$10K/mo (Premium tier only)
- Chargeback risks / financially unstable buyers
- "Just looking" tire-kickers
