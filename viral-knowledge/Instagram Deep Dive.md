On Instagram, there are several different elements you must be aware of, some of which are outlined below.

→ Captions:

For the captions on your posts, reels or stories, you can follow a similar format.

Hook:

Use a question, bold statement, or surprising fact to grab attention.

Example: "Have you ever felt stuck in a rut?"

Provide value:

Use the middle section of your caption to tell a story, offer actionable advice, or share relatable content.

Provide value in one of the three main ways:

1. Documentation
2. Opening Someone's Eyes
3. Education

You can find examples of how Iman does this in the

Instagram Content Library

.

Example:

"When I faced \[problem], I discovered a 3-step method that changed my life, here are some tips \[tips]."

CTA:

Encourage followers to engage.

Example: “Share your thoughts below!”

Use sales-focused CTAs if you’ve already built a relationship with your audience.

"Want to learn more about this, click the link \[link]"

→ Polls and engagement questions:

Use questions or informal polls in your captions and stories to increase interactions.

→ Hashtags:

The role of hashtags has evolved; they now play a critical role in helping the algorithm categorize content.

For newer accounts, use 3-7 hashtags to direct your content to your audience.

(1-2 broad, 2-3 niche-specific, 1-2 community or location-focused).

For more established accounts with 100K+ followers, avoid using hashtags as they can limit your reach.

→ Text in videos:

Add small, subtle text overlays to boost algorithmic engagement while minimizing distractions.

→ Avoid Facebook recommendations:

Turn off Facebook recommendations if your focus is solely on building an Instagram audience.

**Content Strategy**

For your Instagram strategy, always have the 3 C's in mind: content, context and consistency. Just by following this, you will already be ahead of 90% of creators.

1. Content: High-quality, valuable information that shares your journey, solves problems or educates.
2. Context: Understand your audience's needs and desires and deliver the right message for them.
3. Consistency: Follow a regular and predictable posting schedule that your audience can count on.

Post Optimization

Now, let's break down how you can create content that attracts the right audience.

1. Posts

Provide value and establish authority. Instagram posts are the most effective when it comes to evergreen content, testimonials, carousels and brand-building posts. Focus on providing continuous value.

Key Features:

* Static or carousel format.
* Focus on aesthetics and quality.

Structure:

1. Visual:

High-quality photo, infographic, or carousel with tips.

---

2. Caption:

Start with a scroll-stopping line.

Tell a story or educate.

Add a clear CTA.

---

You could also include hashtags to target your reach, especially when getting started.

2. Stories

Use Instagram stories to nurture relationships with your audience and get them to take action.

Story sequences can be a great tool to document your journey and build your personal brand. Additionally, story sequences can be a great way to sell your product or service. In fact, Iman used story sequences to sell many of his Agency programs in the past, for example.

!\[](https://sweetp-user-uploads.s3.eu-west-2.amazonaws.com/stage/39036/pages/996616/8FL%20%2882%29.png)

Below are some key features that allow stories to perform so well.

Key Features:

* Short-lived and exclusive
* Interactivity
* Direct transition of communication into the DMs

Structure:

Story 1: Hook your audience in and introduce what you will talk about

Example: "I'm looking for \[ICP] to \[dream outcome]."

Story 2: Tell the story

"I help \[ICP] achieve \[result]. It doesn't matter if you are just starting out in \[niche] and you have no \[X]."

Story 3: Give proof

"I will show you a way around not having \[limiting belief]." I have helped \[case study]."

Story 4: CTA

Example: "So, for the few ones who want to achieve \[desired outcome], DM me the word \[keyword]."

3. Reels

Instagram Reels allow you to reach a wider audience because the algorithm tends to favour short and engaging videos.

Key Features:

* High discoverability
* Up to 90 seconds long
* Strong focus on trending audio, hooks and fast-paced visuals

Structure:

For Reels, the structure is the same as for stories, as they are both very similar.

A few months ago, Iman has actually started experimenting with a new post type he coined for Instagram Reels, called "Moments". Our Reels have performed extremely well on short-form when it comes to growing your personal brand faster.

**Sales \& Growth**

When it comes to Instagram, there are many ways to grow and leverage your audience for conversions and sales.

The general advice on engagement for growth is as follows:

* Reply to comments with meaningful remarks
* Engage with similar and bigger accounts
* Join conversations in your space
* Build community relationships

But, the best way to actually understand sales and growth on Instagram is to look at how Iman was able to grow year on year by using proven strategies.

These strategies fall into one of six categories, as follows:

→ Providing value

→ Building relationships

→ Cross-platform synergy

→ Mastering features

→ Authentic Collaborations

→ Consistency

Below, you will find a detailed breakdown of this.

1. Providing Value

Iman grew his personal brand effectively by focusing on two key elements: targeted content creation and authentic documentation. He shared insights specifically valuable to entrepreneurs and digital marketers, positioning himself as a thought leader while providing actionable steps for success.

What set him apart was his commitment to transparent, real-time documentation of both his victories and setbacks, which not only built trust and relatability with his audience but also served as an educational window into the realities of brand building.

Together, this combination of practical expertise and authentic storytelling created a compelling narrative that resonated with his followers.

2. Building relationships

Iman built strong connections with his Instagram audience through dedicated two-way engagement and community building. He maintained active communication through comments, stories, direct messages, and live Q\&A sessions, using each touch-point as an opportunity to educate and connect with his followers.

By documenting and sharing his successes and challenges, he created a supportive community where members felt heard and understood. This interactive approach not only kept his existing followers engaged but also encouraged them to organically share his content, helping expand his reach through word-of-mouth.

3. Cross-Platform Synergy

Iman strategically leveraged multiple platforms to amplify his Instagram growth, with YouTube serving as the key traffic driver to his Instagram account. Through educational video content, he demonstrated practical strategies while encouraging viewers to follow his Instagram for additional insights and daily updates.

This cross-platform synergy created multiple touch-points with his audience, allowing him to document his journey across different formats while teaching his followers how to build their own multi-channel presence.

His consistent messaging and content strategy across platforms helped create a seamless experience that strengthened his personal brand and expanded his reach.

!\[](https://sweetp-user-uploads.s3.eu-west-2.amazonaws.com/stage/39036/pages/996616/135.png)

4. Mastering Features

Iman maximized his Instagram presence by strategically utilizing multiple platform features to reach and engage his audience in different ways. He leveraged Stories for behind-the-scenes glimpses and daily updates, IGTV for in-depth educational content and thought leadership, and Reels for trend-based, bite-sized lessons that expanded his reach.

This multi-format approach allowed him to document his journey and share valuable insights in various engaging ways, making his content more accessible and appealing to different audience preferences while building a comprehensive personal brand presence on the platform.

!\[](https://sweetp-user-uploads.s3.eu-west-2.amazonaws.com/stage/39036/pages/996616/136.png)

5. Authentic Collaborations

Iman strategically grew his personal brand through selective brand leveraging and authentic collaborations with fellow thought leaders. Rather than partnering indiscriminately, he chose to collaborate only with creators whose values and messaging aligned with his own, maintaining authenticity in all his partnerships.

These carefully curated collaborations helped expand his reach to new audiences while documenting everything in the process. His authentic approach to partnerships demonstrated to his audience that meaningful collaborations should be based on shared values and genuine connections rather than merely pursuing growth at any cost.

Iman's success in growing his personal brand on Instagram also stemmed from his consistent implementation of a comprehensive strategy that combined multiple tactics. He maintained steady growth through a balanced mix of high-quality content creation, authentic audience engagement, cross-platform promotion, and strategic use of Instagram features.

His ongoing commitment to documenting his journey and educating his followers created a loyal community that appreciates his persistence and authenticity.

Instagram is ideal for those who enjoy visual storytelling and documenting their journey using multiple content formats. Altogether, it is a great tool for building authentic connections and tends to favour newer creators. Below are some additional insights.

**Story Templates**

1. Question \& Answer

* Slide 1: "Quick question for \[target audience]!"
* Slide 2: "\[Industry] poll: \[Option A] vs \[Option B]?"
* Slide 3: "My take on \[topic]: \[insight]"
* Slide 4: "Want more \[value]? DM the word \[word]!"

2. Value Sequence

* Slide 1: "3 ways to \[achieve result]"
* Slide 2: "Step 1: \[action step]"
* Slide 3: "Step 2: \[action step]"
* Slide 4: "Step 3: \[action step]"
* Slide 5: "Save this for later!"

---

**Reel Templates**

**Hook → Content → CTA**

1. Quick Tips

* Hook (0-3s): "The truth about \[topic] nobody talks about"
* Content (3-20s): Share 3 points: "\[Point 1], \[Point 2], \[Point 3]"
* CTA: "Save this if you want to \[achieve result]"

2. Tutorial Style

* Hook (0-3s): "How I \[achieved result] in \[timeframe]"
* Content (3-20s): "Step 1: \[action], Step 2: \[action], Step 3: \[action]"
* CTA: "Follow for more \[topic] tips"

Bonus Template: 'Moments' Framework

Simple Quote: How to become \[X] + Clip

---

**Carousel Templates**

1. Myth-Buster

* Slide 1: "\[X] myths about \[topic] holding you back"
* Slide 2-4: "Myth: \[common belief], Truth: \[reality]"
* Final Slide: "Which myth surprised you? Comment below"

2. How-To Guide

* Slide 1: "The \[timeframe] guide to \[achievement]"
* Slide 2-4: "Step \[number]: \[action step + brief explanation]"
* Final Slide: "Save this \[topic] guide for later"
