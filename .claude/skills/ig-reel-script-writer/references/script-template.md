# Reel Script Template

## Output Format

Every script must be delivered in this format. Adapt section lengths based on target reel duration.

```
TARGET LENGTH: [15s / 30s / 60s / 90s]
HOOK TYPE: [Dream Outcome / Burning Question / Challenge / Controversy / Exposing / Authority / POV / Listicle]
PSYCHOLOGY TRICKS USED: [list the tricks woven into this script]

---

## HOOK (0-3s)
[The one-sentence concept. Must create irresistible curiosity.]

## BUILD-UP (3-8s)
[2-4 sentences that hype the payoff, build suspense, clear objections. Annotate which technique each line uses: Mind Read, Risk Reversal, Authority Endorsement, Controversy, Personal Authority]

## VALUE (8-50s, varies)
[Specific, actionable steps/tips/insights. Every line must serve a purpose. Annotate extreme value moments.]

## PAYOFF
[The resolution to the hook's promise. The moment the viewer gets their answer.]

## CTA (last 2-5s)
[Leverage reciprocity. Prefer CTAs that keep giving over CTAs that just ask.]

---

CAPTION: [Instagram caption with hook line, value summary, and engagement CTA. Include 3-5 relevant hashtags for newer accounts.]
```

---

## Duration Guidelines

### 15-second Reel
- Hook: 1 sentence (2-3s)
- Build-Up: 1 sentence (2-3s)
- Value: 1-2 key points (5-7s)
- Payoff: Built into value
- CTA: 1 sentence (2-3s)

### 30-second Reel
- Hook: 1 sentence (2-3s)
- Build-Up: 1-2 sentences (3-5s)
- Value: 2-3 key points (15-18s)
- Payoff: 1 sentence (2-3s)
- CTA: 1 sentence (2-3s)

### 60-second Reel
- Hook: 1 sentence (2-3s)
- Build-Up: 2-3 sentences (5-8s)
- Value: 3-5 key points or a mini-story (35-40s)
- Payoff: 1-2 sentences (3-5s)
- CTA: 1-2 sentences (3-5s)

### 90-second Reel
- Hook: 1 sentence (2-3s)
- Build-Up: 3-4 sentences (8-12s)
- Value: Full tutorial/story with extreme value (55-65s)
- Payoff: 2-3 sentences (5-8s)
- CTA: 1-2 sentences (3-5s)

---

## Script Annotation Guide

When writing scripts, annotate each section with the psychology tricks being used. This helps the user understand WHY each line exists and learn the craft.

Example annotations:
- `[DREAM OUTCOME]` — painting the viewer's aspirational life
- `[AUTHORITY — subtle proof through setting/results]`
- `[MIND READ — clearing assumed objection]`
- `[RISK REVERSAL — removing barriers]`
- `[EXTREME VALUE — paid-level information for free]`
- `[BURNING QUESTION — unresolved curiosity]`
- `[RECIPROCITY — CTA that keeps giving]`
- `[SCARCITY — limited availability]`

## Example: Full 60-Second Script

```
TARGET LENGTH: 60s
HOOK TYPE: Dream Outcome + Authority
PSYCHOLOGY TRICKS USED: Dream Outcome, Subtle Authority, Mind Read, Risk Reversal, Extreme Value, Actionable Steps, Reciprocity CTA

---

## HOOK (0-3s)
"Day in the life of a TikTok clipper making $20,000 a month."
[DREAM OUTCOME — aspirational lifestyle for target audience]

## BUILD-UP (3-10s)
"Today I woke up to my brother playing Fortnite because I worked way too late last night. So I decided to just sleep in the office. Then I went to go and get a refreshing drink for the morning, then head over to my desk to get started with my shift."
[SUBTLE AUTHORITY — home office setting proves the income claim without bragging]
[DREAM OUTCOME — lazy morning, gaming, working from home = target audience's dream]
[LANGUAGE TRICK — "my shift" implies he works on his own schedule]

## VALUE (10-50s)
"First thing I do is go and search for popular YouTubers to screen record. After watching their video for a minute or two, I end the screen recording and crop the video so that it fits in the screen."
[ACTIONABLE STEPS — specific, followable process]

"Then I go and screen record GTA gameplay to film the cars rolling down the hill and then crop it the same way."
[ACTIONABLE STEPS — continued]

"Then I open up CapCut and start a new project. After importing the first video, you go to overlays to add the second one on top. Then you want to click ratio at the bottom and select the TikTok option."
[ACTIONABLE STEPS — tool-specific, very clear]

"Then you want to go to auto captions and start using THIS format. These settings are going to create the best subtitles for you."
[EXTREME VALUE — showing exact settings, viewers will pause and screenshot]

"Then you want to export your video, go over to your TikTok page and upload it."
[ACTIONABLE STEPS — complete workflow]

## PAYOFF
"One of my videos last month made me $1,200… from one video."
[DREAM OUTCOME — payoff proves the hook's promise with specific proof]

## CTA (last 3s)
"If you want to learn from me and how I do what I do, you can join the free discord in my bio."
[RECIPROCITY — offering MORE free value, not begging for a follow]

---

CAPTION: Day in the life making $20k/month as a TikTok clipper. Full breakdown of my exact process 👆 Want to learn more? Free discord link in bio. #contentcreator #sidehustle #makemoneyonline
```

## Content Format Variants

### Quick Tips Reel
- Hook: "The truth about [topic] nobody talks about"
- Content: Share 3 points concisely
- CTA: "Save this if you want to [achieve result]"

### Tutorial Reel
- Hook: "How I [achieved result] in [timeframe]"
- Content: Step-by-step walkthrough
- CTA: "Follow for more [topic] tips"

### "Moments" Reel (Brand-Building)
- Simple quote + cinematic clip
- Minimal editing, maximum authenticity
- Example: Aspirational quote overlay on a lifestyle clip

### Story-Based Reel
- Hook: Burning question or curiosity gap
- Build-Up: Set the scene
- Value: Tell the story with embedded lessons
- Payoff: The lesson/resolution
- CTA: Reflection or resource offer
