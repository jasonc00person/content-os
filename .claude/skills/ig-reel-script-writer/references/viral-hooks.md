## Hook Format Categories

### Dream Outcome Hooks
Frame the video around the viewer's aspirational lifestyle/result.
- "Day in the life of a [role] making $[amount] a month"
- "How I make $[amount] a month [doing something unexpected]"
- "My [timeframe] [lifestyle] routine as a [role]"
- "All it takes is [short timeframe] to [dream result] and I'm going to prove it"

### Burning Question Hooks
Create genuine curiosity that can only be resolved by watching.
- "Have you ever heard of [concept]?"
- "Is it possible to [unexpected claim]?"
- "What's the [superlative] amount of [thing] you can do and still [result]?"
- "You ever wonder how [famous person] [impressive result]?"

### Challenge/Test Hooks
Frame as an experiment with unknown outcome — creates uncertainty.
- "Testing [thing] part [#]"
- "Everyone says I can [claim], so let's see if that's true!"
- "I tried [challenge] in [timeframe]"
- "Can you [seemingly impossible thing]?"

### Controversy/Bold Statement Hooks
Challenge a belief or make a bold claim that demands attention.
- "I don't know who needs to hear this but [controversial take]"
- "[Person/Company] got [result] by [unexpected method]"
- "[Group] are getting [negative outcome] at alarming rates"
- "I'm sorry, but this mindset will NOT make you successful"

### Exposing/Secret Info Hooks
Promise insider knowledge or behind-the-scenes truth.
- "I'm EXPOSING [person/company]'s [secret/technique]"
- "I finally found it. I found [the secret to something]"
- "Everyone wants to [work at/use X] until you find out…"
- "How much money did [person] make from [event]?"

### Authority/Proof Hooks
Lead with credible results, subtly.
- "Not to flex… but I'm actually [impressive claim]"
- "This is how I took my [thing] from SHIT to [great result]"
- "I'm about to give away my $[amount] [system/strategy] for FREE"
- "[Famous person] just [action] that's gonna [impact]"

### POV/Relatable Hooks
Use shared experiences for instant connection.
- "POV: [relatable scenario]"
- "When you get the [specific relatable situation]…"
- "Have you ever struggled to [common pain point]?"
- "Are you still [outdated thing everyone does]?"

### Listicle/Value-Promise Hooks
Promise specific, countable value.
- "Here are my top [#] [things] of ALL TIME"
- "[#] things you NEED to know about [topic]"
- "I'm gonna save you [time/money] with [#] simple tips"
- "Here's how to [result] in [short timeframe]"

## Hook Templates (Fill-in-the-blank)

### Curiosity-Based
- "Did you know [mistake] could be holding you back?"
- "Why is no one talking about [topic]?"
- "What if I told you [unexpected fact]?"
- "The truth about [topic] no one wants to admit."
- "Everything you thought you knew about [topic] is wrong."

### Pain Point-Based
- "Struggling with [problem]? Here's the solution."
- "If you're doing [problem], STOP right now."
- "Stop doing this if you want [desired result]."
- "Do you know the #1 mistake people make in [niche]?"

### Aspirational
- "Want to [achieve goal] in [time frame]? Here's how."
- "Here's how I achieved [result] in just [time frame]."
- "You're closer to [result] than you think."
- "I wish I knew [solution] 5 years ago."

### Action-Oriented
- "This is your sign to finally [action]."
- "The best time to start is today."
- "Your future self will thank you for doing [action] now."
- "[Solution] changed everything for me."

### Value-Forward
- "[Solution] is the ONLY guide you'll ever need."
- "The step-by-step process for [result]."
- "The only checklist you need to succeed in [topic]."
- "[#] secrets to mastering [skill or habit]."
- "Here's [#] [niche] secrets I wish I knew at [age]."
